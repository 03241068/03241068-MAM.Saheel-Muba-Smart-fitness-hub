Workout Structure

Monday: Likely upper body focus with bench press variations (4 sets 10-15 reps), rows (4 sets 10-15 reps), pull-downs or lat pulldowns (4 sets 10-15 reps), and accessory presses or flies (4 sets 10-15 reps). Wednesday: Mixed or lower emphasis, including squats (4 sets 10-15 reps), bench or chest press (4 sets 10-15 reps), rows or pulls (4 sets 10-15 reps), and deadlift variations (sets 10-15 reps). Thursday: Upper body again, with overhead presses (4 sets 10-15 reps), rows (4 sets 10-15 reps), bench (4 sets 10-15 reps), and pulls (4 sets 10-15 reps). Friday: Lower or full body, featuring squats or deadlifts (sets 10-15 reps), bench (3-4 sets 10-15 reps), and lighter accessories (3 sets 10 reps). ​

Benefits
Such routines build muscle effectively by hitting major lifts 2x weekly for optimal frequency, suiting intermediate trainees like students balancing IT studies. Rest days (Tuesday, Saturday, Sunday) aid recovery, aligning with current date (Saturday rest).Progression Tips
Progression Tips
Increase weight when completing all reps with good form; deload 10% if missing >10% reps over sessions. ​ Warm up progressively, rest 2-3 minutes between sets.
